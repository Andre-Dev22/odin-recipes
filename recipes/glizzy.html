Slow cooker Glizzy

This crockpot glizzy recipe is a warm, easy meal made even easier in the slow cooker. These glizzys may be served in sandwiches or over rice.

    Ingredients
  • 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
  • 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise (Optional)
